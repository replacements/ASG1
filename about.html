About World Training


Established in 2019, World Training always had the dream and goal to go worldwide started in Singapore as a small cozy gym at Yew Tee Community Centre. As the years progressed, World Training has taken the Gym Industry by storm by being an affordable yet reliable and premium gym; having over 6 gyms island-wide as of now. World Training will continue delivering until our goal of being Worldwide is reached.
